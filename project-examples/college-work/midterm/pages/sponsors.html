SPONSORS

The Law Day Run proceeds benefit the Land of Lincoln Legal Assistance Foundation, Inc., and the Belleville Running Club. Land of Lincoln Legal Assistance Foundation provides low income and senior residents of southern and central Illinois with high quality civil litigation services to obtain and maintain their basic human needs. The Belleville Running Club promotes and encourages running as a sport and as a means of healthful exercise in our community; to promote and conduct races or other activities; to disseminate information through newsletters, flyers, presentations, meetings, and educational programs; and to offer opportunities for organized and informal socializing by members.
